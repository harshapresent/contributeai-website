💡 Common Questions
How do I connect Google Calendar?

Go to Integrations and click "Connect Google Workspace". You'll be prompted to authorize access.

How do I book a demo?

Click "Book Demo" in the top navigation or sidebar to schedule a 45-minute session with our team.

What is Google CCAI?

Google Contact Center AI (CCAI) is a suite of AI tools including Dialogflow CX and Vertex AI for automating call center operations.
